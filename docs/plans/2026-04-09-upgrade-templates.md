# Upgrade Figure Skill Templates

**Goal:** Upgrade the template files (`research-template.md`, `distilled-template.md`, `figure-skill-template.md`) used by the `create-figure-skill` workflow to enforce deep research, robust distillation, and strong runtime utility.

**Scope:** Replace the thin placeholder templates with rigorous, humanized, and highly structured schema files. The new templates will enforce evidence citations, explicitly separate observed facts from inferred logic, handle contradictions, and ensure the final skill provides clear cognitive boundaries.

**Why:** The current templates encourage shallow, 5-bullet summaries. A DeepSearch-driven workflow needs thick, source-backed intermediate artifacts to produce a high-quality final skill. This upgrade closes the gap between data collection and final assembly.

**Success criteria:** The three templates are rewritten, human-readable, avoid AI clichés, and mandate detailed source indexing.
